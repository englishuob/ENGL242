Report Writing and Presentation

Theoretical and practical experience in technical report writing and presentation — from the stages of building a report to presenting its findings. The emphasis throughout is on tasks and assignments, the central one being a full-length report delivered formally with PowerPoint.

By the end of this course you should be able to:

  1. Apply standard research methods utilising IEEE format.
  2. Critically analyse and review information from printed and electronic sources in order to paraphrase and summarise effectively.
  3. Apply proper referencing styles and citations, while avoiding plagiarism.
  4. Compose a full-length report utilising correct language.
  5. Articulate research findings using oral presentation skills.
